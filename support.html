FAQ

 Will I need to change my Wiring 

No ,You Can instal this touch switch without any additional wiring .All you require is Normal 2MD metal boxes you can also instal with 6MD and 8MD boxes using additional panel.

 Will I need to have Internet access to run Fhinno ?

No. fhinno works offline, You can operate your switch using touch pannel all the time
and if you wants to operate with app  you just need a wifi router where both the mobile and switch is connected to the same network. But if you want to control the switch from outside the home, you need to have internet on your phone and the home network

 Will there be a fee for the Cloud service 

No the cloud service is completely free

 Will it retain the previous Status after power cut

Fhinno will retain The previous states (before the power cut) when the power is back
